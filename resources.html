What Is Implicit Bias?

Implicit bias refers to unconscious attitudes or stereotypes that affect how we perceive and interact with others.

Intersectionality

Intersectionality describes how different aspects of identity—such as race, gender, class, and ability—overlap and shape lived experiences.
